OpenGraphpaper

About

OpenGrahpaper is a Java program directed at students who struggle with reading their own handwriting(me), often carry a laptop but not paper & pencil and do online math assignments, or disabled students(e.x. broke the arm they write with). OpenGraphpaper is simply a sheet of 150x150 cells, with eaching containing a peice of text. From this simple program, you can easily insert equations, solve them, and write notes about them. It is intuitive to use--for example, it automatically moves to the next cell after typing a character, you can click on a cell to move to it, and the arrow keys will move your highlighted cell.

Github

Simply press the button that says "Clone or download", choose Download as Zip, unzip it, then navigate to the folder called "dist". Finally, double-click OpenGraphpaper.jar and give it 5-15 seconds to open.
