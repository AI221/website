Definite articles in accusative and nominative case

When you have something with der but it is the direct object, it is den. But how do you know if it is the direct object? It's rather simple. If the object is then it is der. For example, Der Pulli is preiswert. The pullover is being cheap, so it is der. However, you can also have Ich kaufe den Pulli. This is den because it being acted upon.

Ein, eine, and einen

As you probably already know, ein is ein when conjugated with der, eine with die, and ein with das. However, when you have den, it is Einen.
